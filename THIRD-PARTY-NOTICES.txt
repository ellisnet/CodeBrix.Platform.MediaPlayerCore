   Project:    https://code.videolan.org/videolan/LibVLCSharp
   Tag:        3.9.7 (commit 6f25c13272c43325d642c1217db077c3efd4c7e5)
   Copyright:  Copyright (C) VideoLAN and the LibVLCSharp authors
   License:    GNU Lesser General Public License, version 2.1 or later

   CodeBrix.Platform.MediaPlayerCore is a fork of LibVLCSharp 3.9.7,
   restricted to the cross-platform managed core (src/LibVLCSharp/Shared).
   Upstream copyright and license headers are preserved verbatim in every
   ported source file.

   The following categories of modification were applied during the port:

     - Namespaces renamed from LibVLCSharp.Shared.* (and sub-namespaces)
       to CodeBrix.Platform.MediaPlayerCore.* . Every ported file's
       namespace line carries a `//was previously: <upstream.namespace>;`
       provenance comment.
     - Target framework narrowed from a multi-TFM matrix to net10.0.
       Conditional-compile branches (`#if NETSTANDARD1_1`, `#if NET40`,
       `#if NETFRAMEWORK`, `#if MONO`, etc.) whose branch would never
       execute on net10.0 were stripped; the live branch was kept.
     - Nullable-reference-type annotations (`string?`, `MyClass?`, etc.)
       on reference types were stripped, because the CodeBrix family
       compiles with NRT disabled. Null-forgiveness `!` operators were
       likewise stripped.
     - Platform-specific `Core.Android.cs`, `Core.Apple.cs`, and
       `Core.UWP.cs` files were omitted; only `Core.Desktop.cs` is
       retained for the net10.0 non-platform-specific library surface.
     - `Platforms/**/*.cs`, `Themes/Generic.xaml`, and
       `Properties/AssemblyInfo.cs` are not ported — all three require
       platform-specific TFMs that the CodeBrix net10.0-only target
       does not include.

   Source availability: the complete corresponding source code for this
   binary distribution is available at
   https://github.com/ellisnet/CodeBrix.Platform.MediaPlayerCore. Copies
   are also available on written request to jeremy@ellisnet.com, valid
   for three years from the date of distribution of this binary.
